About Me

I have graduated in Computer Science at the University of Santiago the Compostela. Coding was one of my biggest passions since I was 14, when I discovered programming through creating and designing games with Unity 3d in C#. Later on I learned PHP and the basics of SQL to make my own websites, and I started becoming more interested in web programming and IT security. In University I have studied a variety of languages such as C, Java, Python, SQL... as well as deeper concepts about software design, database modelling and more. Afterwards, I did a Master in Cybersecurity and worked in a Research Center with Machine Learning and security related topics for over two years. Right now I'm working as a OT security analyst as well in some personal projects.
